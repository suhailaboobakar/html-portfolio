The Best Movies According To Suhail

My Top 3 Movies

The Dark Night Rises

The Dark Knight Rises is an epic conclusion to Christopher Nolan's Batman trilogy, as the caped crusader faces his ultimate challenge against the formidable terrorist leader Bane.

Inception

Inception is a gripping sci-fi heist thriller where a skilled thief must navigate the layers of dreams to implant or extract information, blurring the lines between reality and the subconscious.

Prestige

The Prestige is a mind-bending thriller that explores the intense rivalry between two magicians and their obsession with outdoing each other's greatest tricks.
